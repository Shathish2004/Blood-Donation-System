# App Name: Centralized Blood Donation System

## Core Features:

- Donor Availability Updates: Allow donors to update their availability status and last donation date.
- Individual Blood Request: Enable individuals to request blood, specifying blood type, units, and urgency.
- Hospital Blood Inventory: Enable hospitals to manage blood inventory, including adding, updating, and tracking expiration dates.
- Blood Bank Inventory Management: Enable blood banks to list available blood units and manage blood expiration using AI to estimate expiration dates from stored data.
- Report Analysis Tool: Using LLM as a tool, enable blood banks to analyze hospital blood requests and compare with their inventory reports to identify potential matches.
- Mock ID Verification: Implement a mock verification process for donors, individuals, hospitals, and blood banks using Aadhar ID and license verification.
- Emergency Broadcast Notifications: Enable sending urgent SMS/email notifications to donors and hospitals.
- Donation History Management: Track past donations with details such as date, location, and recipient organization.
- Hospital History Management: Maintain logs of past blood requests, fulfilled units, and emergency polls.
- Redistribution History: Track redistribution activities with details of source, destination, and transferred units.
- Blood Bank History Management: Record past requests, fulfilled distributions, and alerts.
